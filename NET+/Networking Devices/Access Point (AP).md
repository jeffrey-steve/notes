**Function:**  
- Enables wireless devices to connect to a wired LAN

**Key Concepts:**  
- Works at Layer 2  
- Uses Wi-Fi standards (802.11 a/b/g/n/ac/ax)  
- SSID, WPA2/WPA3 encryption

**Types:**  
- Standalone  
- Controller-based

**Common Attacks:**  
- Rogue APs  
- Evil twin  
- Wi-Fi sniffing
